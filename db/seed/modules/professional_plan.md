# Professional Plan

## Overview
The **Professional Plan** is our most popular subscription, designed for small to medium businesses that need a complete suite of tools to run their operations efficiently. It includes everything in the Starter plan plus HR management, payroll processing, basic CRM, and advanced reporting.

## Who Is It For?
- Small businesses with 5–200 employees
- Growing teams that need HR and payroll capabilities
- Companies that want an all-in-one platform instead of multiple separate tools
- Businesses that need CRM to track customer relationships alongside operations

## Key Features
- **Unlimited Projects**: No cap on active projects — manage as many as your team needs.
- **Advanced Time Tracking & Reporting**: Detailed time reports by project, team member, and date range. Exportable to CSV and PDF.
- **Full Invoicing & Billing**: Recurring invoices, automatic payment reminders, multi-currency support, and payment gateway integration.
- **HR Management**: Employee profiles, leave management, attendance tracking, onboarding workflows, and document management.
- **Payroll Processing**: Automated payroll calculations, tax deductions, pay stub generation, and direct deposit support.
- **Basic CRM**: Contact management, deal tracking, and a simple sales pipeline to manage customer relationships.
- **Priority Support**: Email and live chat support with a 4-hour response time during business hours.
- **10 GB Storage**: Ten times the storage of Starter, for all your business documents and files.
- **Team Collaboration**: Shared workspaces, commenting, file sharing, and real-time notifications.
- **API Access**: RESTful API for building custom integrations with your existing tools.

## What Problems Does It Solve?
- Eliminates the cost and complexity of maintaining separate HR, payroll, CRM, and project management tools
- Provides a unified platform where all business operations are connected
- Reduces administrative overhead for growing teams with automated payroll and HR workflows
- Gives managers visibility into team productivity, project status, and customer relationships in one dashboard

## Pricing
- **1–10 users**: $24.99/user/month
- **11–50 users**: $21.99/user/month
- **51–200 users**: $18.99/user/month

## Example Pricing Scenarios
- A team of 15 employees: 15 × $21.99 = $329.85/month
- A team of 50 employees: 50 × $21.99 = $1,099.50/month
- A company of 100 employees: 100 × $18.99 = $1,899.00/month

## Compared to Starter
Everything in Starter, plus:
- Unlimited projects (vs. 5)
- HR management and payroll (not available in Starter)
- Basic CRM (not available in Starter)
- 10× more storage (10 GB vs. 1 GB)
- Priority support with live chat (vs. email only)
- API access for custom integrations

## Upgrade Path
Companies that outgrow Professional typically move to the **Enterprise Plan** when they need SSO, audit logs, dedicated account management, or custom integrations.
