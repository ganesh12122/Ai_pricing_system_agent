# Payroll Add-on

## Overview
The **Payroll Add-on** provides comprehensive payroll processing capabilities. It handles automated payroll calculations, tax filing, direct deposits, and compliance for businesses of any size. Available as an add-on for Starter plan users who need payroll without upgrading to Professional.

## Who Is It For?
- Starter plan users who need payroll processing but don't need the full Professional suite
- Small businesses with basic payroll needs
- Companies operating in multiple states that need multi-state payroll support

## Key Features
- **Automated Payroll Calculation**: Automatically calculate gross pay, deductions, taxes, and net pay for all employees based on their salary or hourly rate.
- **Tax Filing & Compliance**: Automatic federal and state tax calculations. Generates and files required tax forms.
- **Direct Deposit**: Pay employees directly to their bank accounts. Supports multiple bank accounts per employee.
- **Pay Stub Generation**: Professional pay stubs generated automatically with each payroll run, accessible to employees via self-service portal.
- **Multi-state Payroll**: Handle employees across different states with correct state tax calculations and compliance.
- **Year-end Tax Forms**: Automatically generate W-2 and 1099 forms for year-end tax reporting.

## What Problems Does It Solve?
- Eliminates manual payroll calculations and the risk of errors
- Ensures tax compliance across federal and state levels
- Saves time on payroll processing — what used to take hours now takes minutes
- Provides employees with self-service access to their pay stubs and tax documents

## Pricing
- **1–50 users**: $6.99/user/month (on top of base plan)
- **51+ users**: $5.49/user/month

## Compatibility
- Available as add-on for **Starter** plan (since Starter doesn't include payroll)
- **Professional** and **Enterprise** plans already include payroll processing — no add-on needed

## Note
If you need payroll along with HR management, CRM, and advanced project management, the **Professional Plan** includes all of these in one package and may be more cost-effective than Starter + multiple add-ons.
