# CRM Add-on

## Overview
The **CRM Add-on** provides advanced Customer Relationship Management capabilities for businesses that need to track leads, manage sales pipelines, and automate customer follow-ups. Available for Professional and Enterprise plan subscribers.

## Who Is It For?
- Sales teams that need to manage leads and deals
- Businesses transitioning from spreadsheets to a proper CRM
- Companies that want their CRM integrated with project management and invoicing
- Teams managing ongoing client relationships

## Key Features
- **Lead Tracking & Scoring**: Capture leads from web forms, email, or manual entry. Automatic lead scoring based on engagement and fit criteria.
- **Sales Pipeline Management**: Visual pipeline with customizable stages (Lead → Qualified → Proposal → Negotiation → Closed). Drag-and-drop deal management.
- **Automated Follow-up Emails**: Set up email sequences that trigger automatically based on deal stage, time elapsed, or custom conditions.
- **Contact Management**: Centralized contact database with full interaction history, notes, and tags.
- **Sales Analytics & Reports**: Pipeline reports, win/loss analysis, revenue forecasting, and team performance dashboards.
- **Email Provider Integration**: Connects with Gmail, Outlook, and other email providers for seamless email tracking.

## What Problems Does It Solve?
- Eliminates scattered spreadsheets and sticky notes for tracking customer relationships
- Automates repetitive follow-ups so no lead falls through the cracks
- Provides sales managers visibility into pipeline health and team performance
- Keeps all customer interactions in one place, accessible to the entire team

## Pricing
- **1–50 users**: $7.99/user/month (on top of base plan)
- **51+ users**: $5.99/user/month

## Compatibility
- Works with **Professional** and **Enterprise** plans
- Not available with Starter plan (Starter includes no CRM capabilities; upgrade to Professional for basic CRM, or Professional + CRM Add-on for advanced CRM)
