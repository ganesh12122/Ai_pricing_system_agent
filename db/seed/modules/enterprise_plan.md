# Enterprise Plan

## Overview
The **Enterprise Plan** is our premium subscription built for large organizations that require maximum control, security, compliance, and dedicated support. It includes everything in the Professional plan plus enterprise-grade features like SSO, audit logs, custom integrations, and a dedicated account manager.

## Who Is It For?
- Large organizations with 50+ employees
- Companies in regulated industries that need compliance and audit capabilities
- Organizations that require Single Sign-On (SSO) for security
- Businesses that need custom integrations with their existing enterprise software stack
- Teams that need guaranteed uptime SLAs and 24/7 support

## Key Features
- **Everything in Professional Plan**: All features from Professional are included.
- **Single Sign-On (SSO)**: SAML 2.0 and OAuth 2.0 support for seamless integration with your identity provider (Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace, etc.).
- **Audit Logs & Compliance**: Complete activity audit trail for all users. Exportable logs for compliance reviews. Supports SOC 2 and GDPR compliance requirements.
- **Custom Integrations**: Our engineering team works with you to build bespoke integrations with your existing ERP, HRIS, or financial systems.
- **Dedicated Account Manager**: A named account manager who understands your business and provides proactive guidance.
- **24/7 Phone Support**: Round-the-clock phone support in addition to email and chat, with a 1-hour response time for critical issues.
- **Unlimited Storage**: No storage limits — store all your documents, files, and backups without worrying about space.
- **Advanced Security Controls**: IP whitelisting, two-factor authentication enforcement, session management, and data encryption at rest.
- **Custom Workflows**: Build custom approval workflows, automation rules, and business logic tailored to your organization's processes.
- **SLA Guarantee**: 99.9% uptime guarantee with financial credits for any downtime.

## What Problems Does It Solve?
- Meets enterprise security and compliance requirements (SSO, audit logs, encryption)
- Provides dedicated support for complex deployments and migrations
- Enables custom integrations that connect our platform to your existing enterprise systems
- Removes all limits on storage and usage so organizations can scale without constraints
- Guaranteed uptime SLA for business-critical operations

## Pricing
- **1–100 users**: $49.99/user/month + $500/month platform fee
- **101–500 users**: $44.99/user/month + $500/month platform fee
- **501+ users**: $39.99/user/month + $1,000/month platform fee

## Example Pricing Scenarios
- A company of 75 employees: (75 × $49.99) + $500 = $4,249.25/month
- A company of 200 employees: (200 × $44.99) + $500 = $9,498.00/month
- A company of 1000 employees: (1000 × $39.99) + $1,000 = $40,990.00/month
