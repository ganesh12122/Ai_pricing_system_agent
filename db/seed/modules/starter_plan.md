# Starter Plan

## Overview
The **Starter Plan** is our entry-level subscription, perfect for individuals, freelancers, and solopreneurs who need basic tools to manage their day-to-day work without the complexity of enterprise software.

## Who Is It For?
- Freelancers and independent contractors
- Solo entrepreneurs managing their own business
- Small teams (up to 10 people) who need basic project tracking
- Anyone who needs simple invoicing and time tracking

## Key Features
- **Project Management**: Create and manage up to 5 active projects with task lists, deadlines, and basic kanban boards.
- **Time Tracking**: Log hours against projects and tasks. Generate simple timesheet reports.
- **Basic Invoicing**: Create professional invoices from tracked time, send them to clients, and track payment status.
- **Email Support**: Access our support team via email with a 24-hour response time guarantee.
- **1 GB Storage**: Store project documents, invoices, and attachments.

## What Problems Does It Solve?
- Eliminates the need to juggle multiple free tools for project management, time tracking, and invoicing
- Provides a single, unified dashboard for all your basic business operations
- Helps freelancers track billable hours accurately and invoice clients professionally

## Pricing
- **1 user**: $9.99/month
- **2–10 users**: $8.99/user/month
- **11–50 users**: $7.99/user/month

## Limitations
- Maximum 5 active projects
- No HR or payroll features (available as add-ons or in Professional plan)
- No CRM capabilities (available as add-on or in Professional plan)
- Basic reporting only (advanced analytics available as add-on)
- 1 GB storage limit (extra storage available as add-on)

## Upgrade Path
Users who outgrow the Starter plan typically upgrade to the **Professional Plan** when they need unlimited projects, HR management, payroll, or CRM capabilities.
