# Analytics Add-on

## Overview
The **Analytics Add-on** provides business intelligence and advanced analytics capabilities. Create custom dashboards, generate detailed cross-module reports, and gain actionable insights from your data. Available for all plan subscribers.

## Who Is It For?
- Business owners who want data-driven decision making
- Managers who need detailed reports on team performance, project health, or financials
- Companies that need scheduled reports delivered to stakeholders
- Any user who wants more than the basic built-in reports

## Key Features
- **Custom Dashboards**: Build drag-and-drop dashboards with charts, tables, and KPIs from any module in the system.
- **Advanced Reporting Engine**: Create complex reports with filters, grouping, calculated fields, and conditional formatting.
- **Data Export**: Export reports and dashboards to CSV, PDF, and Excel formats for sharing with external stakeholders.
- **Scheduled Reports**: Automatically generate and email reports on a daily, weekly, or monthly schedule.
- **Real-time Metrics**: Live-updating dashboards that reflect the latest data across all your modules.
- **Cross-module Analytics**: Combine data from projects, HR, payroll, CRM, and invoicing into unified reports.

## What Problems Does It Solve?
- Replaces manual report creation in spreadsheets with automated, always-up-to-date dashboards
- Gives executives a single view of business health across all operations
- Saves hours of manual data compilation and reporting
- Enables proactive decision-making with scheduled alerts and reports
